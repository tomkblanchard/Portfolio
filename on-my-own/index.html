UX Case Study

On My Own

Mobile app for people with disabilities to learn and practice independent living skills

Role

Lead UX Designer: Research, Ideation, Wireframing, Visual Design (Individual Project)

Time Frame

May 15, 2020 - August 01, 2020

Tools

Figma, Pen & Paper, Whiteboard

Overview

Project Brief

On My Own (OMO) is a mobile app that aims to help people with disabilities to learn and practice independent living skills. While it is designed for people with disabilities, it is also helpful to anyone who wants to gain independent skills.

Problem

Gaining the neccessary skills to live independently can be challenging for many people. It can often be even more challenging for people with disabilities. While hands on learning and instuction in this area is extremely useful, tools that empower people with disabilities to self direct this process are limited.

Goal

The goal is to develop a mobile app that allows users to learn and practice independent living skills, set self defined goals and rewards, and track their progress on their journey to independent living.
